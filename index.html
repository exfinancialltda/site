How We Work

A clear and transparent process for your operations.

  1. 1

    Chat With Us & Sign Contract

    Initiate the process by talking to our team. We'll clarify all your doubts and formalize our partnership with a service contract.

  2. 2

    Make a Transfer

    After signing, you will receive the details of our receiving account to make your transfer securely.

  3. 3

    Receive Your Money & Documents

    Once the transfer is confirmed, you provide your receiving data and get your money. We will issue an official invoice (Nota Fiscal) for the transaction.

  4. 4

    Bank Compliance

    If your bank questions the origin of the funds, you can provide the official government-issued documents about your transfer for full transparency.
